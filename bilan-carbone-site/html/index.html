Why Measure Your CO2 Emissions?

Measuring your carbon footprint is the first step toward making a positive environmental impact. Our calculator helps you identify which aspects of your lifestyle contribute most to climate change – from transportation and housing to food choices and consumption habits. Armed with this knowledge, you can make targeted changes that reduce emissions while often saving money and improving your quality of life. Climate action starts with awareness, and every reduction in CO2 emissions contributes to a healthier planet for future generations.
